Process Catalogue

Editorial Designer
Adobe InDesign, Photoshop
University of Iowa School of Art
[ Add project image here ]

Overview

Process Catalogue is a 40-page print publication designed for the University of Iowa School of Art. The catalogue showcases student and faculty artwork alongside written reflections on creative process — bridging fine art and design in a single cohesive volume.

The Brief

Create a print-ready catalogue that highlights the artistic process — not just the final works — for students and faculty at the School of Art. The publication needed to feel professional and gallery-quality while remaining accessible and student-centered.

Process

  • Collaborated with contributors to collect artwork, photography, and written statements
  • Developed a grid system and typographic hierarchy in Adobe InDesign
  • Curated and color-corrected imagery in Photoshop for print accuracy
  • Designed cover, section openers, and interior spreads across 40 pages
  • Prepared print-ready files with bleeds, crop marks, and color profiles

Design Approach

  • Clean, editorial grid that lets artwork breathe without feeling sparse
  • Neutral color palette with deliberate accent colors per section
  • Type pairing: serif display for headers, clean sans-serif for body copy
  • Consistent margin and gutter system across all 40 pages

Outcome

The final catalogue was printed and distributed through the University of Iowa School of Art. This project demonstrates my ability to manage a large-scale print design project from concept through press-ready production.
