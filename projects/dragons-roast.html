Academic / Personal Project

Overview

Dragon's Roast is a brand identity project for a fictional Vietnamese-fusion coffee shop. The concept merges Vietnamese cultural heritage with modern specialty coffee aesthetics — creating a brand that feels both rooted and contemporary.

The Challenge

Design a cohesive brand identity from scratch that could believably represent a Vietnamese-fusion café competing in a specialty coffee market. The brand needed to communicate warmth, cultural pride, and quality without falling into stereotypes.

Process

  • Developed a brand brief defining target audience, tone, and positioning
  • Researched visual references across Vietnamese art, typography, and coffee branding
  • Sketched and iterated on logo concepts before moving into Illustrator
  • Built out a full brand system: logo, color palette, typography, and usage guidelines
  • Assembled final deliverables in Penpot for presentation

Outcome

The final brand system includes a primary logo, alternate lockup, color palette, type hierarchy, and a one-page brand guidelines sheet. The project demonstrates end-to-end brand development from concept to deliverable.
